Frequently Asked Question

General Questions

  • what is a Leo Club?

    The Leo Club serves as a youth-oriented organization, empowering its members to partake in leadership growth, community service, and networking, all while contributing positively to society

  • What kinds of service projects do Leos participate in?

    Leos engage in a diverse array of service projects, addressing Sustainable Development Goals and Lions' global service challenges. These encompass environmental efforts, educational assistance, health awareness initiatives, and providing aid to those in need.

  • How can I become a Leo Club member?

    To join the Leo Club, simply locate a local chapter in your community, express your interest, and become an active participant in their activities and meetings.

  • What age group can join the Leo movement?

    The Leo Club welcomes young individuals aged 12 to 30, nurturing youthful energy and providing a platform for personal development and community service.

  • What are the benefits of being a Leo?

    Embracing Leo membership brings the chance for leadership growth, personal development, community enrichment, valuable networking, and international acclaim for exceptional contributions.
